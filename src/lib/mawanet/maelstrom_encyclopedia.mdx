# Maelstrom Encyclopedia Central

**Maelstrom Encyclopedia Central** (MAEC, reads as _Mace_) is a database (more precisely it's snapshot) with a wiki-like frontend containing structured information extracted and compiled from Transcribers Arrays <Link path="/mawanet/maelstrom_watchtowers_network">ATA</Link> and <Link path="/mawanet/maelstrom_watchtowers_network">RWTA</Link> of the <Link path="/mawanet/maelstrom_watchtowers_network">Watchtower</Link>.

It's informational/memetic/narrativistic and conceptual payload is compartmentalised by the snapshot and kept isolated within the <Link path="/mawanet/narraphysic_isolation_environment">narraphysic isolation environments</Link>. The knowledge obtained from the database and subsequently awareness of it is realised only within the confinements of said <Link path="/mawanet/narraphysic_isolation_environment">narraphysic isolation environment</Link> wherein you are currectly located.

In case of a security breach, your <Link path="/mawanet/conceptual_configuration">conceptual configuration</Link> will be forcefully altered in order to purge any signs of narrativistic/narraphysic/memetic/conceptual awareness of the database, it's narremes, and corresponding concepts.

## Structure

The MAEC database consists of interconnected sections each corresponding to a time period or an age retroactively defined by a paramount change within <Link path="/mawanet/erkholl">Erkholl's</Link> <Link path="/mawanet/narraphysic_realm">narraphysic realm</Link>. These changes are required to be proven to be present casually **or** paracasually within planet's altered chronology. They are refered to as *paradigms changes/shifts*.

An age between two adjacent *paradigm changes* is named after suspected potential <Link path="/mawanet/narrative">metanarrative</Link> whose <Link path="/mawanet/conceptual_configuration">potential to exist</Link> is defined by an accumulation of history within said age and culminating as the next adjecent paradigm change. As with any <Link path="/mawanet/retroactive_refractory_feedback_loop">retroactive positive feedback loop</Link>, these <Link path="/mawanet/narrative">narratives</Link> are discovered *post factum* but are predicted and corrected before their potential <Link path="/mawanet/conceptual_configuration">conceptual configuration</Link> had fully arrived into present -- that is before the narrative is completed it could be redefined by an Actor aware of it's imminent completion within observable futures.

Database snapshots are generated periodically as well as automatically when <Link path="/mawanet/maelstrom_watchtowers_network">the Watchtower</Link> detects a sign of any significant <Link path="/mawanet/superimposition_event">superimposition event</Link> or a <Link path="/mawanet/narrative">narrative</Link> taking place across <Link path="/mawanet/narraphysic_realm">narraphysic realm</Link> of the <Link path="/mawanet/standard_reality">standard reality</Link>.
